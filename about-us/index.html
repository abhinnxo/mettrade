Who We Are?

Mettrade/Mcorio stands as a vanguard within the realm of digital supply-chain innovation, poised to redefine the landscape of metal commerce. Our focused expertise in metals positions us as a pivotal entity in the industry, offering a sophisticated platform as the nexus for harmonious interactions between metal suppliers and discerning consumers. Our unwavering commitment is channeled into the meticulous development of a universal marketplace, characterized by the virtues of expeditious and transparent transactions. We function as orchestrators, meticulously choreographing a logistics ballet to guarantee a seamlessly orchestrated end-to-end procurement experience. Beyond mere startup status, we pride ourselves as pioneers, heralding a transformative era in the domain of metal procurement.

Our Values

One of the main goals of Mettrade is to become the metals community's one-stop shop and pricing information platform, as well as to offer state-of-the-art logistical solutions for effective procurement. Our mission is to set the bar for professionalism in the services we offer, while also providing the market with excellent value and a wonderful, trustworthy experience for each and every one of our clients.

Our Global Presence

Discover metal excellence at Mettrade, where innovation meets industry in three dynamic locations. In Pittsburgh, we forge connections in the Steel City, offering a hub for seamless transactions and competitive pricing. Brampton, Ontario, sees Mettrade expanding borders, bringing a global network to your doorstep with efficiency at its core. In Delhi, our hub in the heart of India delivers a transformative metal procurement experience with transparent transactions and cutting-edge logistics.

About us

Mettrade is a Full-stack Digital Supply-Chain platform that integrates global network of steel suppliers & provides the customers with the best prices in the Market. Mettrade also provides the customers with customization & processing of steel products as per their need, making it a One-Stop Solution in the market.
